Study In Australia

Australia

Why Choose Australia

Australian education is respected internationally when seeking employment or further study at leading international universities. Distinguishing Australia from many other countries is the Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) which is a national system of leading pathways recognized throughout Australia and many other countries. Degrees from Australia are globally recognized. People in Australia are very friendly and co-operative. Weather is very suitable for Indian students. Post Qualification work & settlement options are also available in the country. You can work while studying. Universities are known for being research focused & technology driven. 


    General Facts

    Details

    Capital: Canberra

    Population: Approximately 22 million.

    Size: Approximately 7.6 million square meters

    Time Zone: Australia has three different time zones, the time difference between Australia and Europe can vary between +6 to +10 hours

    Currency: Australian Dollar (AUS $)

    Official language: English

Education System


Australia has 41 universities and all of them are routinely accessed and evaluated by governmental authorities to ensure a high education standard. Students are very involved in the review process.

Studying in Australia, you can opt for both undergraduate as well as postgraduate degrees. An undergraduate degree normally takes up to three years to complete and a postgraduate degree takes another one to two years. Many universities also offer an Honours Degree, a year of specialization, once an undergraduate degree has completed by a student.

Students can also gain a postgraduate diploma after completing the undergraduate degree which is known as a one-year postgraduate degree. For international students, the best option considered is to study in Australia for one or two semesters (Semester Abroad program. Australian universities are known for the excellent LL.M. programs in a variety of specialist fields.


Admission Process


Admissions are totally based on a student's academics & IELTS score. While applying for admissions one must submit the following documents along with the application:

  • Marksheets & Passing certificate of Year 10 & Year 12
  • IELTS Result Copy (If not given- conditional offer letter will be issued)
  • Marksheets of bachelor's degree
  • Degree Certificate (Provisional degree certificate is acceptable if original not received till date)
  • Marksheets & Degree certificate for PG/master’s degree, if completed in India.
  • Work Experience certificates (if applicable)
  • Statement of purpose/ Recommendation letters (Depending on course)
  • Application fees draft (Depending on the university)

Visa Application Process


In Australia, a student visa is required for any stay longer three months. Stays over 12 months require an additional medical examination by a panel doctor of the Australian government. Working Holiday Visa is another option if you are opting for your semester abroad. However, this visa has a variety of restrictions which must be taken care of.
